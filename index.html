About Us

Our Mission | Vision & Plans

Our Mission at Save Oxygen Foundation is to contribute towards a greener and healthier planet by planting trees and promoting sustainable practices. Through our efforts, we aim to combat the rising levels of air pollution and climate change, which pose a threat to the very existence of life on earth. We believe that planting trees is one of the most effective ways to tackle environmental challenges, and we are committed to planting as many trees as possible in our local communities and beyond. By doing so, we not only create more oxygen and reduce carbon dioxide in the atmosphere, but also provide habitats for diverse species of animals and promote soil health. At Save Oxygen Foundation, we are dedicated to educating people about the importance of trees and their role in maintaining ecological balance. We organize workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to spread our message and encourage more people to join us in our mission. Our ultimate goal is to create a world where humans and nature can coexist in harmony, and we believe that planting trees is a crucial step towards achieving this. Join us in our mission to Save Oxygen and make the world a better place for future generations.
